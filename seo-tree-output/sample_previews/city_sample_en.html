Why India for Medical Treatment?

1. World-Class Medical Infrastructure

India's top hospitals are internationally accredited (JCI, NABH) and equipped with the latest medical technology. Fortis, Narayana Health, and Apollo Hospitals in Bangalore are recognized globally for excellence in cardiac care, orthopedics, oncology, and fertility treatment.

2. Highly Qualified Doctors

Indian doctors are trained at premier institutions worldwide (USA, UK, Europe) and have extensive experience treating international patients. Many specialize in procedures relevant to Saudi patients and understand regional health concerns.

3. Significant Cost Savings

Medical procedures in India cost 60-70% less than in Saudi Arabia, without compromising on quality. A heart bypass surgery costs $10,000-15,000 in India compared to $50,000+ in Riyadh. These savings cover travel, accommodation, and treatment combined.

4. Complete Arabic Support

All our partner hospitals provide:

  • Arabic-speaking medical coordinators available 24/7
  • Professional Arabic translators for all consultations
  • Medical reports translated to Arabic
  • Halal food options and prayer facilities
  • Culturally sensitive care for Saudi patients

5. Easy Travel from Riyadh

Bangalore is just 4-5 hours from Riyadh with multiple direct flights daily on Saudia, IndiGo, and Air India. The e-Medical Visa process is quick (3-5 days) and Shifa AlHind handles all paperwork for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is medical treatment in India safe for Saudi patients?

Yes, absolutely. India's top hospitals are internationally accredited (JCI, NABH) and maintain the same quality standards as leading hospitals in Saudi Arabia, the USA, and Europe. Over 2 million international patients visit India annually, with high satisfaction rates.

Will I have language barriers in India?

No. All our partner hospitals provide Arabic language support including Arabic-speaking doctors, professional translators, and Arabic medical reports. English is also widely spoken in Bangalore's medical facilities.

How long do I need to stay in India?

Stay duration depends on the procedure:

  • Heart surgery: 10-14 days
  • Joint replacement: 7-10 days
  • IVF treatment: 21-28 days
  • Dental implants: 5-7 days
  • Eye surgery: 3-5 days
We help arrange extended stay if needed for recovery.

Can my family accompany me?

Yes. We can arrange medical attendant visas for family members, and our accommodation options include family suites near the hospital.
